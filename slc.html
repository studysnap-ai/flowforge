Common questions

Before you reach out.

The questions every SLC business owner asks — answered straight.

Can't I just use an AI marketing tool instead?
You can — and some are decent at social posts. But they can't build you a website that converts, fix a broken Google Business Profile, write real Spanish, or pick up the phone when something breaks.

AI tools sit on top of whatever you already have. If it's broken, they just automate the problem. We fix the foundation first.
How long does a project take?
A Launch website is typically done in 2–3 weeks from the day you approve the plan. One point of contact, no committees, no waiting.
Do I have to pay everything upfront?
No. It's 50% to start, 50% when we deliver. We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and Stripe. Monthly plans start after the site is live.
What if I already have a website?
We'll look at it on the free audit call and tell you honestly: fix what you have, or start fresh. Sometimes a few targeted changes are enough.
Do you actually write in Spanish, or just translate?
We write it. There's a real difference between translated corporate Spanish and copy that sounds natural to a Spanish-speaking community in Utah. We're bilingual — we know the difference.
What do I own at the end?
Everything. Code, domain, hosting account, Google Business Profile access — all handed over to you on delivery. No lock-in, ever. It's yours. Full stop.
