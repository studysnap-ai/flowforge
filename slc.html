Common questions

Before you reach out.

The questions every SLC business owner asks — answered straight.

Can't I just use an AI marketing tool instead?
You can — and some of them are decent at scheduling social posts and writing generic captions. But here's what they can't do: build you a website that actually converts visitors into calls. Fix a Google Business Profile sending people to the wrong address. Write content in real Spanish that sounds like a human, not a translator. Or pick up the phone when something breaks.

AI tools sit on top of whatever you already have. If what you already have is broken or missing — they just automate the problem. We fix the foundation first. Then we automate on top of something that works.
How long does a project take?
A Launch website is typically done in 2–3 weeks from the day you approve the plan. We move fast because we keep the process simple — one point of contact, no committees, no waiting. You'll see progress every few days, not once a month.
Do I have to pay everything upfront?
No. It's 50% to start, 50% when we deliver. That protects both sides and keeps things moving. We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and Stripe. Monthly plans start after the site is live.
What if I already have a website?
We'll look at it on the free audit call and tell you honestly: fix what you have, or start fresh. Sometimes a few targeted changes are enough. If your current site is too slow, too old, or built on a platform that limits you, we'll tell you that too — and explain why.
Do you actually write in Spanish, or just translate?
We write it. There's a real difference between translated corporate Spanish and copy that sounds natural to a Spanish-speaking community in Utah. We're bilingual — we know the difference, and your customers will too.
What do I own at the end?
Everything. Code, domain, hosting account, Google Business Profile access — all handed over to you on delivery. No lock-in, no monthly platform fee, no "you can only edit it through us." It's yours. Full stop.
