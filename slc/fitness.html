Common questions

What gym owners ask us.

Can't I just use Mindbody or Glofox for my website?
Those are booking management tools — not websites. They give you a booking widget you embed somewhere, but you still need an actual site that represents your brand, ranks on Google, and gets people to that booking step in the first place. We build that site.
Do you integrate with my existing booking software?
Yes. We integrate with Calendly, Mindbody, Acuity, and most other booking tools. If you don't have one, we'll set up a simple booking form that works without any monthly software fee.
How long does it take?
2–3 weeks from the day you approve the plan. One point of contact — you're talking directly to the person building it. No account managers, no waiting.
What if I already have a website I don't like?
We'll tell you honestly on the free audit call: targeted fixes, or start fresh. Sometimes a new hero section and a proper booking flow is all you need. We'll know after 20 minutes.
