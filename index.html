Common questions

Before you reach out

The questions every client asks — answered upfront.

How long does a project take?
Depends on scope. A landing page takes 3–5 days. A Chrome extension 1–2 weeks. An AI integration 1–3 weeks. We give you a clear timeline before we start — no surprises.
Do you require a deposit?
Yes — 50% upfront to start, 50% on delivery. This protects both sides and keeps the project moving. We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and Stripe.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
We include revision rounds in every project and work with you until it's right. We don't close a project until you're satisfied — that's our standard, not a policy.
Can you work with a small budget?
Yes. Our starting prices reflect real, lean work — no bloated agency overhead. If your budget is tight, tell us and we'll scope something that fits. We'd rather start small than not work together at all.
Do I own everything after delivery?
100%. All code, assets, and accounts are handed over to you at the end of every project. No lock-in, no monthly fees unless you want ongoing support.
Do you work with clients outside the US?
Absolutely — most of our clients are international. We work across time zones, communicate in English and Spanish, and handle remote projects end to end.
